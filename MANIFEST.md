## Meaning of “canonical” here

This repo is the canonical **handoff package**.

Within it:

- the most current **language/design docs** are under `docs/spec/v1-rc0`
- the most current **runtime implementation** is `packages/runtime`
- the most current **browser persistence layer** is `packages/bridge`
- the most current **IDE implementation** is `packages/core`, `packages/vscode`, and `packages/jetbrains`
- older slices remain preserved for context and provenance under `archive/`
